Computer Vision Lab

We do research on automatic analysis of visual data such as images, videos, and 3D/4D visual sensors. We operate on the threshold of signal processing and machine learning, focusing on deep learning in particular. Example applications include object and action recognition, human behavior analysis, medical imaging.
